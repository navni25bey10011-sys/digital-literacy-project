# Stay Safe Online Checklist

- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Do not click on suspicious links in emails or messages  
- Always verify the sender before sharing any information  
- Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords with anyone  

- Do not approve unknown UPI payment requests  
- Always double-check UPI IDs before sending money  

- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or transactions  
- Regularly check bank statements for unauthorized activity  
- Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store or App Store  

## Reporting Cybercrime

- Report incidents at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
